Navigating the Heat of Online Conversations
Flaming: Online 'fighting' by posing negative messages with anger and/or vulgarities on message boards (such as those on gaming sites or blogs).
Flaming, or online roasting, involves posting offensive insults, often due to the anonymity of internet forums. It differs from trolling, which aims to cause discord.
Factors like anonymity, lack of social cues, and reduced accountability contribute to flaming. Flamers intentionally provoke and specialize in targeting specific aspects of controversial discussions.
