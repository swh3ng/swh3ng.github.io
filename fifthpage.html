Helpful Self-Help Hotlines

Samaritans Of Singapore

Dedicated suicide prevention agency offering emotional support to those in crisis, including issues arising from online interactions. Their services can provide help and guidance to individuals affected by the negative impacts of online flaming and cyberbullying.

Counselling and Care Centre

Provides professional counseling services that address a range of issues, including the emotional distress caused by online flaming. Offer support and strategies to individuals impacted by cyberbullying and online harassment.

Singapore Association for Mental Health

The Singapore Association for Mental Health offers support and resources to individuals affected by the mental and emotional challenges of online flaming and cyberbullying.

The Institute of Mental Health in Singapore provides counseling and mental health services to individuals dealing with the psychological effects of online flaming and cyberbullying.
